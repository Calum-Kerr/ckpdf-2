About Office to PDF Conversion

Converting Office documents to PDF offers several advantages:

  • Ensures consistent formatting across different devices and platforms
  • Creates smaller file sizes for easier sharing
  • Preserves document layout, fonts, and images
  • Makes documents viewable without Microsoft Office
  • Provides better security and prevents unwanted editing

Supported Document Types

Word Documents
  • DOC (Word 97-2003)
  • DOCX (Word 2007+)
  • Preserves text, images, tables, and formatting
Excel Spreadsheets
  • XLS (Excel 97-2003)
  • XLSX (Excel 2007+)
  • Preserves cells, formulas, charts, and formatting
PowerPoint Presentations
  • PPT (PowerPoint 97-2003)
  • PPTX (PowerPoint 2007+)
  • Preserves slides, animations, and formatting
