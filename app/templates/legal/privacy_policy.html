Last updated: 14/04/2025

Introduction

RevisePDF ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website revisepdf.com and use our PDF processing services.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the site.

Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us when you:

  • Upload PDF files for processing
  • Contact us via email
  • Subscribe to our newsletter

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Process and complete transactions
  • Send you technical notices and support messages
  • Respond to your comments and questions

File Security and Privacy

All files uploaded to RevisePDF are processed on our secure servers. We do not permanently store your files unless explicitly stated for a specific service. Files are automatically deleted after processing or after a short period (typically 24 hours).

We do not access, view, or analyse the content of your files except as necessary to provide our services.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our website and hold certain information. Cookies are files with a small amount of data which may include an anonymous unique identifier.

For more information about our use of cookies, please see our Cookie Policy.

Data Retention

We will retain your information only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this privacy policy. We will retain and use your information to the extent necessary to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our policies.

GDPR Compliance

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain data protection rights. For more information about our GDPR compliance, please see our GDPR Compliance page.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
