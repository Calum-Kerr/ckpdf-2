About RevisePDF

Our Mission

RevisePDF was created with a simple mission: to provide high-quality, accessible PDF tools for everyone. We believe that working with PDF documents should be easy, efficient, and free from technical barriers.

What We Offer

Our platform offers a comprehensive suite of PDF processing tools, including:

  • Optimisation: Compress, repair, and OCR your PDFs
  • Conversion: Convert to and from PDF format
  • Editing: Add page numbers, watermarks, and edit content
  • Organisation: Merge, split, extract, and rotate pages
  • Security: Unlock, protect, redact, and flatten PDFs

All of our tools are designed with user experience in mind, making it easy for anyone to work with PDF documents regardless of technical expertise.

Our Technology

RevisePDF is built using modern, open-source technologies:

  • Python: Our backend is powered by Python, a versatile and powerful programming language
  • Flask: We use the Flask web framework for its simplicity and flexibility
  • PyMuPDF: For core PDF processing capabilities
  • Advanced Processing Engine: For high-quality PDF optimisation and conversion
  • Tesseract OCR: For optical character recognition
  • Bootstrap: For a responsive, mobile-friendly user interface

Privacy & Security

We take your privacy and security seriously. All files uploaded to RevisePDF are processed securely and deleted after processing. We do not store your files or their contents, and all processing is done on our secure servers.

For more information, please see our Privacy Policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, feedback, or suggestions, please don't hesitate to contact us. We're always looking for ways to improve our service and would love to hear from you.
